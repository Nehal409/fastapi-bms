Project Description

AI based BMS for Electric Vehicle

Our project ensures that the battery is protected at all times during charging and discharging and any operation out of its safety limit is prevented.

In this project we used fuzzy logic, an artificial intelligence algorithm, to demonstrate the improvement in SoC estimation accuracy. It safeguards both the user and the battery by ensuring that the cell operates within its safe operating parameters.
